Salad Kiwi Sugga

Amir Samantha

Ingredient

  • 1 kiwi fruit
  • 1 large Fuji apple
  • 1 medium red dragon fruit
  • 5 strawberries

Material for Dressing

  • Mayonnaise to taste (me: cheese-flavored maestro)
  • Plain yogurt to taste (me: chimory)
  • to taste Sweetened condensed milk
  • to taste Grated cheese

Manufacturing Steps

  1. Wash all the fruit, then cut it according to taste, put it in a medium size container, for fruit depending on taste, I just depend on the contents of the fruit in the refrigerator.
  2. Combine Mayonnaise, sweetened condensed milk & yogurt. Stir until smooth, then check the taste and thickness. Then pour it into the cut fruit, add the grated cheese on top, and the strawberry and kiwi pieces for sweetening.
  3. Put it in the refrigerator until it's cold, and it's ready to be enjoyed.
